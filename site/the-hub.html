Innovation & Infrastructure

The UK electric vehicle transition continues to accelerate rapidly, yet infrastructure visibility and charging consistency remain fragmented across regions. Consumers, fleet operators, and manufacturers still face uncertainty surrounding charging reliability, regional infrastructure maturity, and real-world EV usability beyond laboratory testing environments.

StratumEV interprets these evolving market conditions through a structured intelligence-led framework focused on infrastructure behaviour, charging ecosystems, vehicle positioning, and long-term adoption confidence. Rather than analysing specifications in isolation, the platform evaluates how vehicles interact with real-world charging conditions, regional infrastructure availability, and broader market dynamics across the UK.
