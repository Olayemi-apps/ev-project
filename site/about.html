What we do

We combine curated OEM specifications with sourcing context. You can compare models, review trims, and request sourcing using a structured brief.

  • Model briefs: specs, trims, last-checked source date
  • Comparison: side-by-side headline metrics
  • Concierge request: pre-filled sourcing email with your requirements

Why compliance-led matters

UK sourcing can fail on details: documentation, variant differences, charging compatibility, and market-specific specs. We design the journey around feasibility first.

  • Reduce avoidable delays through upfront clarity
  • Focus on usable charging and platform capability, not brochure peaks
  • Prioritise verified sources and freshness
