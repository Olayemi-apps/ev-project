What “compliance-led” means

We evaluate vehicles against UK-specific constraints first: charging hardware and protocols, documentation, type approval signals, warranty/servicing realities, and ownership friction points.

  • Charging compatibility: ports, AC/DC standards, network behaviour, and practical charge curves.
  • Import documentation: provenance, compliance docs, recall status, and data-backed spec verification.
  • Road legality and IVA risk: the right indicators before you commit capital.
  • Ownership practicality: servicing, warranty, parts, OTA behaviour, and insurance friction.
