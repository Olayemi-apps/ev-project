FAQ

What is “UK compliance-led sourcing”?

It means decisions are guided by UK import feasibility, documentation, and real-world charging usability, not just marketing figures.

Is the data official?

Model briefs are built from official sources and updated via ingest snapshots, with the last-checked date shown on each model page.

Can you help me source a specific trim?

Yes. Use “Request sourcing” on the model brief and include your target trim, budget range, and delivery timeline.
